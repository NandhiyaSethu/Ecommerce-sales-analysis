# Key Findings

## 1. Furniture Margin Problem
- Sales: $742K | Profit: $18K | Margin: 2.5%
- Tables sub-category loses $17K despite $206K sales
- Recommendation: Review supplier costs

## 2. Central Region Discounting
- Avg discount: 24% vs 15% other regions
- Profit margin: 7.9% — lowest of all regions
- Recommendation: Cap discounts at 15%

## 3. Seasonal Sales Pattern
- Q4 peaks every year — Nov 2017 hit $116K
- Jan-Feb consistently lowest months
- Recommendation: Stock inventory in September
